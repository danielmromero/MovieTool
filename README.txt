Daniel's Movie Tool

Open the app by double-clicking:
  Open Daniel's Movie Tool.command

What it does:
- Starts a small local web server on your Mac
- Opens Daniel's Movie Tool in your default browser
- Lets you refresh a live movie and TV catalog for Netflix, Hulu, HBO Max, Disney+, Paramount+, Peacock, Prime Video, Apple TV+, and other major streaming services
- Supports Movies / TV Shows / Both filtering, multi-genre filtering, min and max IMDb rating and vote filters, year and runtime filters, and CSV export
- Shows larger posters, type badges, multi-service availability, and JustWatch popularity rank

Notes:
- The first refresh may take a little while because it is gathering live catalog data.
- The app uses JustWatch's public-facing GraphQL endpoint. It is suitable for personal use, but because it is unofficial, it could change.
- Some very large catalogs can hit the public endpoint cap. The interface will flag that when it happens.
- TV season counts depend on what the upstream catalog exposes for each title.

To stop the local server:
  Stop Daniel's Movie Tool.command
